Napoleon's House



Napoleon House in New Orleans is a charming and atmospheric spot that
perfectly captures the city’s old-world character. With its weathered
walls, antique décor, and laid-back courtyard, it feels like stepping
back in time. The food and drinks—especially the classic Pimm’s
Cup—add to its warm, inviting vibe, making it a favorite for both
locals and visitors. It’s the kind of place where history, comfort,
and great flavor all come together effortlessly.


It's also healthy!
